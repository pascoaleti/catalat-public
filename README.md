## What Catalat is

Catalat is a SaaS platform for **catalog websites**.

It was designed for businesses that need to publish products, vehicles, properties or service catalogs on a **fast public website**, manage everything through a **simple private panel**, and convert visitors through **WhatsApp, contact forms, phone or direct negotiation**.

This is not a traditional ecommerce project.

The product is built for operations where the buyer usually wants to:

- compare options
- ask questions
- request availability
- negotiate details
- close the deal through conversation

That makes Catalat especially useful for local businesses, sales teams, brokers, service operators and contact-first catalog workflows.

## Why it exists

Many businesses do not need carts, stock logic, shipping rules and heavy ecommerce flows.

They need:

- a professional catalog website
- clean item pages
- structured categories
- SEO-friendly public pages
- a simple admin panel
- a domain they control
- a faster path from visit to message

Catalat exists to solve exactly that.

## What makes Catalat different

- **Catalog-first architecture**
  Built for presentation and lead capture, not checkout complexity.

- **Simple admin**
  Customers can manage items, categories, pages, blog posts, domain settings and contact information without needing a developer for routine changes.

- **Direct conversion**
  Public pages are optimized to move visitors into WhatsApp, direct contact or negotiation flows.

- **Custom domains**
  Customers can use their own domain or start with a generated address.

- **Multilingual experience**
  The public site and the customer panel support multiple languages.

- **Performance-oriented public layer**
  The storefront is designed to stay lightweight, indexable and fast.
